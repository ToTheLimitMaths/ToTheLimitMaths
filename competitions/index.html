Competitions

To The Limit Maths Competitions (TTLMC) hosts annual math competitions which typically consist of 15 mid AMC 10 to AIME level problems that need to be solved in ~90 minutes. These competitions are great preparation for the American Math Competition Series and even have hundreds of dollars of prizes. Currently, we are hosting a contest on February 5th with $325 in awards. Read more below and sign up here!


2022 Competition

Our 2022 competition is LIVE! Register here: tothelimitmaths.org/2022.

  • Timing: 3:00 - 5:10pm PST (10 minute buffer) via Zoom
  • Date: February 5th, 2022
