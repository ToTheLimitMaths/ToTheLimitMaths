As A Tutor I Allow For:

  • My name and role in the organization to be public
  • My email to be contacted
  • My nationality known to the board

REQUIRED: If these are violated once, you will be removed from the tutors of To the Limit Maths, as well as your school being contacted.

  1. Once logged in to the official TTLM social media accounts, I will stick to common sense and use it only for TTLM beneficiary purposes.
  2. If myself or another tutor is cheating (such as putting extra hours) I will report them to a Head Tutor.
  3. I will not publicize any private information about the organization that would be presented to me.

WARNING: The first violation will result in a warning, and the second violation will result in being removed from the tutors of To the Limit Maths (you can re-apply).

  1. All communication between myself and my student will be through email/discord.
  2. I will stay on topic during tutoring sessions and all other related meetings.
  3. I will not exchange contact information with any student unless specifically asked by the student or I have permission from a Head Tutor.
  4. I will check my TTLM email every day, and if my student sends me an email, I will respond within 24 hours.
  5. I will respond to Emails and Discord announcements promptly.
  6. I will ask the student to fill out the feedback form after tutoring (Feedback Form Link)
  7. I will not use my email for non-TTLM purposes.
